# How to Find the Test Users Section

## You're Currently On: OAuth Overview Page

You're seeing the OAuth Overview/Metrics page. You need to go to the **OAuth consent screen** configuration page.

## Steps to Get to Test Users:

### Option 1: From Current Page
1. Look at the **left sidebar** (navigation menu)
2. Under **"Google Auth Platform"** section, you should see:
   - OAuth Overview (you're here)
   - **OAuth consent screen** ← Click this!
3. Click **"OAuth consent screen"**

### Option 2: Direct Navigation
1. In the left sidebar, click **"APIs & Services"**
2. Then click **"OAuth consent screen"**

### Option 3: Search
1. Use the search bar at the top (where it says "Search (/) for resources...")
2. Type: `OAuth consent screen`
3. Click on the result

## Once You're on OAuth Consent Screen Page:

You should see one of these:

### Scenario A: Setup Wizard (First Time Setup)
- You'll see steps like:
  - **Step 1: App information**
  - **Step 2: Scopes**  
  - **Step 3: Test users** ← **ADD YOUR EMAIL HERE**
  - **Step 4: Summary**

### Scenario B: Already Configured
- You'll see:
  - **"EDIT APP"** button at the top
  - Publishing status showing "Testing"
  - **"Test users"** section below (with "+ ADD USERS" button)

## If You Still Don't See Test Users:

1. Click the **"EDIT APP"** button (usually top right or top of page)
2. This will take you through the setup steps
3. Navigate to **Step 3: Test users**
4. Click **"+ ADD USERS"**
5. Enter: `yolxanderjaca@gmail.com`
6. Click **Add**
